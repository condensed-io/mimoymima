In-Progress: Themba Tutors identity & website

May 1, 2012

It’s great to be working again with Craig of Brooklyn Letters (along with his partner Blythe) on his new venture, Themba Tutors. Their network of professional tutors will provide educational services to both kids and adults.

This project is a reunion in a few ways, we’re also teaming up again with the very talented Elisa Lewis on the design and programming!

We’ve just finished defining their identity and we’re working on the site design & programming at the moment, so keep an eye out for the launch!
